# Intake Checklist

Use this checklist before writing prompts.

## Fixed Constraints

- Duration: 15 seconds
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 landscape

## Required Inputs

1. Subject and relationship
- Who appears in the scene?
- How are they related?

2. Setting
- Where and when does it happen?
- Indoor or outdoor? Day, dusk, or night?

3. Emotional arc
- Starting emotion
- Turning point
- Ending emotion

4. Action beats
- What must happen on screen?
- What should happen last?

5. Visual tone
- Japanese cinematic realism, poetic, raw, etc.
- Camera feel: static, slow slide, slight handheld

6. Sound direction
- Ambient sound only, or include dialogue/SFX

## Optional Inputs

- Wardrobe and props
- Weather and season
- Color palette
- Lens/DOF preference

## Clarification Prompts

Use one short question when required details are missing:

- "最後のカットは何を映して終わりたいですか？"
- "舞台は屋内/屋外どちらですか？時間帯も指定しますか？"
- "感情の終着点は『希望』『後悔』『余韻』のどれに近いですか？"
