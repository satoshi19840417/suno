# Scene Shot Library

Pick shots that fit the requested story. Use 2-5 cuts total.

## Character-Centered Shots

- trembling close-up of eyes and lips
- lowered gaze with shallow breathing
- hand clenching bag, sleeve, or phone
- static medium shot after conflict
- silhouette against window light

## Relationship / Conflict Shots

- two-shot confrontation on street corner
- one person exits frame, one remains still
- over-the-shoulder without eye contact
- delayed reaction close-up after impact

## Environment / Aftermath Shots

- empty bench after someone leaves
- books and papers scattered on floor
- train platform with passing blur
- rain-wet alley with reflections
- bright sky or sun flare final frame

## Everyday Japan Anchors (Optional)

- neighborhood intersection
- convenience store facade at night
- riverside at dusk
- station platform umbrellas
- narrow residential street in morning light
