# Style Tags

Use a small set of tags that fit the story.

## Core Tags

- cinematic, Japanese film tone, restrained emotion
- natural light, shallow depth of field, subtle film grain
- deliberate camera movement, contemplative pacing

## Optional Atmosphere Tags

- warm morning light
- cloudy daylight softness
- rainy street reflections
- dusk glow
- backlit silhouette

## Optional Emotion Tags

- quiet grief
- restrained anger
- emotional endurance
- unresolved tension
- bittersweet aftertaste

## Sound Tags

- gentle ambient sound
- no dialogue
- distant city noise
- wind and fabric rustle
