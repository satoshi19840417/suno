# Example Prompts (Bilingual)

Use these as reference patterns. Keep output as one main prompt unless variants are requested.

## Example 1: Quiet Daily-Life Sadness

### Japanese
```text
15秒、16:9横長、24fps。日本映画調、自然光、浅い被写界深度、抑制された感情。Cut 1 (0.0-4.0s): 駅前の夕方、行き交う人々の中で主人公だけが立ち止まる。Cut 2 (4.0-8.5s): スマホ画面を見た瞬間、表情が曇り、視線が落ちる。Cut 3 (8.5-12.5s): 鏡越しの顔アップ、涙をこらえて呼吸を整える。Cut 4 (12.5-15.0s): 窓辺の逆光、静かな余韻でフェードアウト。台詞なし、環境音のみ。
```

### English (Paste into Sora2)
```text
15-second video, 16:9 landscape, 24fps, Japanese cinematic realism, natural light, shallow depth of field, restrained emotion, no dialogue, ambient sound only. Cut 1 (0.0-4.0s): At dusk near a station, people pass by while the protagonist stands still. Cut 2 (4.0-8.5s): She checks her phone and her expression drops as her gaze lowers. Cut 3 (8.5-12.5s): Mirror close-up, she controls her breathing and holds back tears. Cut 4 (12.5-15.0s): Backlit window frame and quiet lingering fade-out.
```

## Example 2: Conflict and Separation

### Japanese
```text
15秒、16:9横長、24fps。日本映画調、夕方の街角、手持ちを抑えたカメラ。Cut 1 (0.0-5.0s): 若いカップルが向かい合い、緊張が高まる。彼女が彼の頬をビンタする。Cut 2 (5.0-10.0s): 彼女の涙のアップ。背を向けて立ち去る。Cut 3 (10.0-15.0s): 彼がその場に残り、下を向いてうつむく。最後は静かにフェードアウト。台詞なし、遠い車音と風のみ。
```

### English (Paste into Sora2)
```text
15-second video, 16:9 landscape, 24fps, Japanese cinematic realism, dusk street corner, restrained handheld motion, no dialogue, ambient city noise and wind. Cut 1 (0.0-5.0s): A young couple faces each other and tension rises; she slaps his cheek. Cut 2 (5.0-10.0s): Close-up on her tearful face as she turns away and walks out of frame. Cut 3 (10.0-15.0s): He remains alone, lowers his gaze, and stands in silence; end with a soft fade-out.
```

## Example 3: Endurance and Light

### Japanese
```text
15秒、16:9横長、24fps。日本映画調、柔らかな逆光、粒子感のある質感。Cut 1 (0.0-5.0s): 若い女性の涙目アップ、顔はくしゃくしゃだが崩れない。Cut 2 (5.0-9.5s): 一度下を向き、涙を落とさないように耐える。Cut 3 (9.5-13.0s): ゆっくり空を見上げ、輪郭が逆光で光る。Cut 4 (13.0-15.0s): 太陽の眩しさで画面を満たし、白飛び気味に終わる。台詞なし、風と呼吸音。
```

### English (Paste into Sora2)
```text
15-second video, 16:9 landscape, 24fps, Japanese cinematic realism, soft backlight, subtle film grain, no dialogue, wind and breathing ambience. Cut 1 (0.0-5.0s): Close-up of a young woman with tearful, crumpled expression, trying not to break down. Cut 2 (5.0-9.5s): She looks down and fights to keep tears from falling. Cut 3 (9.5-13.0s): She slowly looks up toward the sky, edges glowing in backlight. Cut 4 (13.0-15.0s): Bright sun fills the frame with strong highlights and near-overexposed fade.
```
